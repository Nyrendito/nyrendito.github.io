Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2026

Foreman ("we," "us") operates the Foreman app and the related quote and invoice web pages (together, the "Service"). This policy explains what we collect, why, and who we share it with. It is written in plain English.

Who this policy is for

Foreman has two kinds of people:

  • The pro — the tradesperson who uses the iOS app to create quotes and invoices.
  • The customer — the homeowner who opens a quote or invoice from a text link to accept, sign, and pay.

This policy covers both.

What we collect

Account information (pro). Your name, email address, and a user identifier when you sign up with Sign in with Apple or email and password.

Business information (pro). Your business name, logo, license number, price book, quote defaults, and reminder settings — the details that appear on your letterhead and documents.

Audio recordings (pro). When you record a job, we collect the audio so we can transcribe it and build a quote. Recording requires your permission to use the microphone.

Quote and job content (pro). The quotes, invoices, line items, exclusions, and photos you create or attach.

Customer contact information (entered by the pro). The names, email addresses, and phone numbers of the customers you quote and invoice.

Customer activity (customer). When a customer opens a quote or invoice web page, we record that it was viewed, the signature they draw when they accept, and the status of any payment they make.

Subscription status (pro). Whether you have an active subscription, trial, or renewal. This is handled through Apple In-App Purchase and RevenueCat. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

We do not collect precise location, contacts from your device address book, health data, or browsing history, and we do not use third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

Why we collect it

We use the information only to provide the Service:

  • to create your account and sign you in;
  • to turn your recordings into priced, branded quotes;
  • to send quotes and invoices to your customers and let them accept, sign, and pay;
  • to send automatic payment reminders on the schedule you choose;
  • to manage your subscription and unlock paid features;
  • to keep the Service secure and to provide support.

We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with third parties for their advertising.

Service providers we share data with

We use a small number of providers to run the Service. They only process data on our behalf, for the purposes above:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and file storage (account data, quotes, invoices, photos, signatures).
  • Deepgram — converts your job recordings into text (transcription).
  • Anthropic — structures the transcript into a quote (the text of your recording is processed to produce line items).
  • Stripe — processes customer deposit and invoice payments on the web. Stripe handles card details directly; we do not store them.
  • RevenueCat and Apple — manage your auto-renewable subscription and purchase status.
  • Resend — delivers the emails we send on your behalf, including quotes, invoices, and payment reminders.

Each provider is bound to protect the data and use it only to deliver its part of the Service.

How long we keep it

We keep your account and business data for as long as your account is active. Quotes, invoices, and customer records are kept so your business history stays intact. Audio recordings are kept to support the quotes they produced; we remove them when they are no longer needed for that purpose. When you delete your account, we delete your personal data, subject to any limited records we must keep to meet legal, tax, or accounting obligations.

Deleting your account

You can delete your account at any time from inside the app, under Settings. Deleting your account removes your personal data from the Service as described above.

Your choices

  • You can review and edit your business information, price book, and customer records in the app.
  • You can turn off automatic payment reminders per invoice or in Settings.
  • You can revoke microphone access in your device's iOS Settings (you will not be able to record new jobs until you grant it again).
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription in your Apple account settings.

Children

Foreman is a business tool intended for adults. It is not directed to children and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy we will update the effective date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.
