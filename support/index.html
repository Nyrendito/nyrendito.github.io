Frequently asked

How do I create a quote?
Open Foreman, tap the record button, and talk through the job the way you'd explain it to your crew — rooms, materials, coats, the works. Foreman turns it into priced line items on your branded letterhead. Review it, adjust anything, then send the link to your customer by text or email.
My customer got a link — how do they accept and sign?
They tap the link, which opens the quote in any web browser. They can read the full quote, then tap Accept & sign, draw their signature with a finger, type their name, and confirm. You'll see the quote flip to Accepted. No app or account needed on their end.
Does my customer need to download anything?
No. Quotes and invoices open as a normal web page on their phone or computer. Foreman the app is only for you, the pro.
How do payments work?
When card payments are enabled on your account, your customer can pay a deposit or invoice balance directly from the quote or invoice page. Payments are processed securely by Stripe — card details never touch your phone or ours. If online payment isn't set up, the page shows the amount due and asks the customer to arrange payment with you directly.
How do automatic reminders work?
When you send an invoice you can set a reminder schedule. Foreman emails the customer on that schedule until the invoice is paid, so you don't have to chase. You can turn reminders off per invoice or in Settings at any time.
Why does Foreman need my microphone?
So you can record a job and have it transcribed into a quote. That's the whole point. You can revoke microphone access in your iOS Settings, but you won't be able to record new jobs until you grant it again.
How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Foreman Pro is an auto-renewable subscription billed through Apple. Manage or cancel it any time in your Account Settings in the App Store (Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your iPhone). See our Terms for the full subscription details.
How do I delete my account and data?
Open the app, go to Settings, and choose to delete your account. This removes your personal data from the Service as described in our Privacy Policy, subject to any limited records we must keep for legal, tax, or accounting reasons.
Is my customers' data safe?
Yes. We don't sell data or use it for advertising. We share it only with the providers needed to run the Service (hosting, transcription, email, payments). The full list is in our Privacy Policy.
